Simple present

Explanation

We use this time for indicate actions that happens in our present

Nosotros usamos este tiempo, para indicar acciones que suceden en nuestro presente

Grammar rules

Subject + verb + complement

Sujeto + verbo + complemento

Examples

Affirmative(Afirmativo)

I eat the breakfast

Yo como el desayuno

Negative(Negativo)

You don't eat the breakfast

Tu no comes el desayuno

Interrogative(Interrogativo)

Do you eat the breakfast?

多Tu comiste el desayuno?

Present Continuous

Explanation

We use this time for indicate actions that happens in this moment

Nosotros usamos este tiempo, para indicar acciones que esten pasando en este momento

Grammar rules

Subject + verb to be + verb(-ing) + complement

Sujeto + verbo "to be" + verbo(-ing) + complemento

Examples

Affirmative(Afirmativo)

You are studying

Tu estas estudiando

Negative(Negativo)

You aren't studying

Tu no estas estudiando

Interrogative(Interrogativo)

Are you studying?

多Tu esas estudiando?

Present Perfect

Explanation

We use this time for indicate events that happens recently

Nosotros usamos este tiempo, para indicar eventos que han sucedido recientemente

Grammar rules

Subject + verb to have + + verb in past participle complement

Sujeto + verbo "to have" + + verbo en pasado participio complemento

Examples

Affirmative(Afirmativo)

She has visited Oaxaca

Ella ha visitado Oaxaca

Negative(Negativo)

She hasn't visited Oaxaca

Ella no ha visitado Oaxaca

Interrogative(Interrogativo)

Has she visited Oaxaca?

多Ha ella visitado Oaxaca?

Present Perfect Continuous

Explanation

We use this time for indicate actions that happens in the present and it haven't ended

Nosotros usamos este tiempo, para indicar acciones que esten pasando en el presente y aun no concluyen

Grammar rules

Subject + verb to have + been + complement

Sujeto + verbo "have" + "been" complemento

Examples

Affirmative(Afirmativo)

He has been singing

El ha estado cantando

Negative(Negativo)

He hasn't been singing

El no ha estado cantando

Interrogative(Interrogativo)

Has he been singing?

多Ha estado el cantando?

Explanation

We use this time for indicate actions that happened in a lapse of time in the past

Nosotros usamos este tiempo, para indicar acciones que sucedieron en un lapso de tiempo en el pasado

Grammar rules

Subject + was/were + verb(-ing) + complement

Sujeto + "was/were" + verbo(-ing) + complemento

Examples

Affirmative(Afirmativo)

I was talking with my brothe today

Negative(Negativo)

Yo no estaba hablando con mi hermano hoy

Past Perfect

Explanation

We use this time for indicate actions that happened before another action in the past

Nosotros usamos este tiempo, para indicar acciones que pasaron antes de otra accion en el pasado

Grammar rules

Subject + had + verb in past participle + complement

Sujeto + "had" + verbo en pasado participio + complemento

Examples

Affirmative(Afirmativo)

I had studied english

Yo habia estudiado ingles

Negative(Negativo)

I hadn't studied english

Yo no habia estudiado ingles

Interrogative(Interrogativo)

Had you studied english?

多Habias tu estudiado ingles?
